WELCOME TO THE HOURGLASS TWINS

These two planets constantly fill each other with sand in an infinite loop

Apparently, the sand came originaly from Ash Twin, although it is not safe to say.

EMBER TWIN

This planet is where the nomai from one of the scape pods set their most advanced settlement.

The only problem was, of course, the sand, which often made it difficult for them to move around the planet.

The surface of the planet is hot enough to kill anything on it, but under it, the air is fresher, and it is reasonably confortable to live.

And so, they built the Sunless City inside a cave system in the north hemisphere of the planet.

There, they built the High Energy Lab.

There, they achieved the creation of artificial black holes and white holes in order to generate vasts amounts of energy, in addition to gain the technology to warp travel.

These two warp cores are home to compressed white and black holes

ASH TWIN

This planet is where the nomai decided to build the warp towers to teleport instantly to any of the other important locations for their projects

The main problem, as in Ember Twin, was the sand, which only made the building proccess harder

These towers, each representing their designated warp location, were carved out of rock, and contained a black hole warp core to suck the traveler when they were on the warp platform and the tower aligned with it's destination core.

These platforms, black hole and white hole, correspondingly, would be tangled together with nomai technology, meaning that they would only pair between them.

The nomai also created a station that would orbit the sun awating a signal to fire and destroy it.

This station is a marvel of engineering, but only the most expert pilots can board it unless the Sun Tower is used to warp there.
